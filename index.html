About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm James Sheehan! I am a Biomedical & Software Engineer with a primary focus in Brain-Computer Interfaces, Neuroengineering, and Human-Computer Interactions. I completed my Undergraduate degree at the University of Vermont, engaging in experiences ranging from manipulating brain-computer interfaces to developing full scale software applications.

While at university I was heavily involved in neuroscience mobile EEG research at the Glass Brain Laboratory. Working with a team of students under Dr. David Jangraw, our team attempted to incorporate virtual reality and AR elements to traditional mobile EEG study designs in an attempt to study the brain's response in a more naturalistic environment.
